ABOUT

Kay Video Productions is the creative studio of filmmaker John Kopanski, specializing in emotionally grounded, visually striking films and videos that leave an impression. With a background in both narrative storytelling and commercial work, John's projects are driven by the mentality to make it honest, and make it matter.

His directing style is collaborative but precise, bringing together the best crew of talent that fits each specific project individually. If you're an artist, a brand, or a producer with an ambitious idea, KVP can bring it to life with precision, style, and absolutely zero bullshit.
